I'm a web developer and instructor
based in Ann Arbor, Michigan.

I'm a web developer.

I graduated from a Grand Circus bootcamp in June 2017 and have been coding ever since. Currently, I work at DocNetwork here in Ann Arbor. We created an app to help protect the health and safety of kids at camps and schools all over the country.

I'm a teacher.

As an Instructor with both Girl Develop It - Ann Arbor and Grand Circus - Detroit, I help students from all backgrounds take their first steps into the world of code in a fun and accessible way. I specialize in low-barrier workshops for complete beginners. I am also available for speaking engagements at conferences.

I'm a feminist.

My work has truly given me a passion for educating and empowering other women and minorities in tech. A diverse workplace is a successful workplace. I'm honored to be able to mentor women all over the greater Detroit and Ann Arbor areas to help them break into tech, and to be able to learn from the wonderful people I've met along my way.
